Institutional Heritage

Holy Grace Group of Educational Institutions came into being in 1999 under the banner ‘Holy Grace Foundation’. Affiliated with the University of Kerala, we provide a holistic learning environment that balances rigorous academics with cultural values.

Located away from the city's hustle, our eco-friendly campus offers the perfect atmosphere for intellectual growth and research.

Admission Info

Process

Admission via University of Kerala Centralized Allotment Process (CAP). UG selection based on merit; PG largely through entrance exams.

Eligibility

Minimum 45% aggregate in Higher Secondary or equivalent for UG. Graduation in relevant field for PG.
